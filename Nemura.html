NEMURA

  • Name: Nemura

  • Species: Mermaid (Origin Form), Harpy (Altered Form)

  • Biological Age: 26

  • Gender: Female

  • Height: 6'9" ft / 206cm

INFORMATION

A Mermaid whose sense of adventure is almost as strong as her sleepy personality, she originally knew little outside of the sea she lived in, not knowing what lay past its sheer cliffs. With the power of a special dream pearl she found at the bottom of the sea one day, she's able to transform into a harpy and soar across the sky. She eventually came to reside at Worldview, where she travels to other worlds to find the perfect place for her next name. While her blank expression and near zen-like state might make one underestimate her, her reflexes are surprisingly fast when in danger, and knows how to fight as well. While an airheaded person as one might expect, she can have her moments where she can reveal just how intelligent she actually is.

TRIVIA

  • The original design for Nemura Origin Form was a full Mermaid with a full tail with no legs. This was changed to better match the Altered form.

  • The term Origin Form and Altered Form is a reference to Pokemon, where the Creation Trio each has a form with the same name.

  • Besides being called her Origin and Altered Forms, unofficially they’re also referred to as her Surf and Turf Forms, which can be seen in a lot of the file names or her drawings.

  • The original idea for Nemura would have had her based off of Sakura’s and have a pink color pallet. This was changed to the more blue-green pallet she'd always had before a draft of a pink design was made. This idea would later be used for Somei instead.

  • Nemura’s name comes from ‘nemu’ which is sleep in Japanese, and ‘sakura’, a remnant of her original cherry blossom theming.

  • She is known to hold her hand up to her face a lot. The reason for this is unknown.

  • The concept for a character that can transform between an aquatic form and an Aireal form that would turn into Nemura was inspired by the Hippogryphs and Seaponies of My Little Pony.

  • She’s also based on Nemu from That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime: Trinity in Tempest, which is where her sleepy personality and holding her hand to her face come from.

  • Her Altered Harpy form is also based on the Harpys in Reincarnated as a Slime, which is notable for having separate wings on their backs.

  • Despite Aranie’s distance from most people, she considers Nemura a friend and the only person on Worldview she feels she can truly talk to.

  • The pearl she wears is able to transform her due to it being her deepest wish by the either it absorbs. If removed, Nemura will be stuck in what ever from she currently is in.
